Find hidden objects in lush jungle scenes

Free Puzzle iPhone & iPad 4+

Step into wild jungle environments and sharpen your observation skills. Lost The Jungle Hidden Object is a calm yet focused seek-and-find adventure: every scene hides cleverly placed items, and the clock keeps you on your toes. Use hints when you need a nudge, earn stars by finishing before time runs out, and work your way through handcrafted levels drenched in nature detail.

What you do in the game

  • Search richly illustrated jungle and nature scenes for listed objects.
  • Race gentle timer challenges to stay sharp and unlock stars.
  • Lean on the hint system when an item proves especially elusive.
  • Progress through multiple levels at your own pace—relaxing or intense.

Highlights

  • Handcrafted hidden-object levels with a jungle and outdoors theme.
  • Timer-based rounds that reward focus without feeling rushed.
  • Hints to keep the flow going when you are stuck.
  • Clean interface and smooth controls built for touch on phone and tablet.
  • Game Center support including leaderboards.
  • Ideal for fans of seek-and-find, observation puzzles, and nature vibes.
